Why Use TextCasePro?

Whether you're writing an email, coding, or preparing a document, consistent text formatting is crucial. Accidentally left Caps Lock on? Need to capitalize titles for a blog post? TextCasePro handles it all with a single click.

Available Conversions:

  • Sentence Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence. Perfect for standard writing.
  • Lower Case: Converts all characters to lowercase. Useful for cleaning up data.
  • Upper Case: Converts all text to capital letters. Great for headers or emphasis.
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of every major word. Ideal for headlines and titles.
